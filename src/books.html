A Mathematical Introduction to Logic
Herbert Enderton
Math 414 at UIUC 		The text begins by breaking down mathematics to its axiomatic expressions and begin foundational logical arguments. The text explains how to create logical expressions and how these logical expressions can follow from others. Without these rules for logical expressions, many proofs would have no foundation and could collapse due to a logical fallacy. The end goal of this text is to prove Godel's Completeness Theorem.
