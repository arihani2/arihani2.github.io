Self Studying

The text begins by asking readers what logic is and how it applies to mathematics. Once establishing a need for a foundational language that logical statements can be derived from, the text then proceeds to establish this language. Throughout the text, the reader learns how to create logical expressions and the rules and consequences that follow. The end goal of the text is to prove Godel's Completeness Theorem.
